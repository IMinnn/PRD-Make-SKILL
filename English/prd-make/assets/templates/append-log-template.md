# Append Log

## Instructions

- Record one log entry after every PRD append, revision, confirmation, skip, or rollback.

- "Review Status" marks whether the current content has been confirmed by the user, such as `Pending Confirmation`, `Confirmed`, `Needs Adjustment`, or `Auto Continue`.
- "Notes" records the reason for the change, user confirmation basis, impact scope, or next step.
