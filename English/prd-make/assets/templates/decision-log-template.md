# Decision Log

## Instructions

- This file centrally records items that are not yet decided during PRD writing and review and require continued confirmation from product, technical, or business stakeholders.
- Pending decisions are not written into `PRD Document.md` or feature module details.
- Closed items remain in this file and are marked through the status field; do not delete them from the record.
- Record only questions that affect product behavior, engineering scope, data models, permissions, flows, priorities, or acceptance criteria.
- Current handling/default assumptions can only be temporary handling for moving the writing forward; they must not be treated as user-confirmed conclusions.
- After the user confirms an item, update its status and close time. If needed, synchronize revisions to `PRD Document.md`, `Module Plan.md`, or `Append Log.md`.
- IDs use consecutive `D001`, `D002` numbering and must not be mixed with feature IDs, business rule IDs, or acceptance criteria IDs.
